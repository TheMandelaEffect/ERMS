East Rand Milk Suppliers (Pty) Ltd

Services:

We specialise in delivering fresh milk to companies. Our delivery vehicles start operating from Midnight to ensure that our clients milk is delivered before their staff start work.
Daily deliveries are accompanied with a invoice and a statement is issued monthly. There are over 500 corporate clients we service daily, references are available on request. Retail (shops) are serviced on a small scale.

Products:

We package our milk under the brand Oak Tree Dairy. All our milk is pasteurized, homogenized and clarified.
Product range includes:

Long Life, Full cream and low fat milk in 2L Jugs, 1L Bottles, 500ml bottles and 1L sachets. Cream 1L Bottles.
